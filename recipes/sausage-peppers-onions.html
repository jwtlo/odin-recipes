Sausage, Peppers, and Onions

Description

This is one of my go-to recipes for any day of the week. As flavorful as it is colorful, you cannot go wrong with this recipe!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pack of sausages
  • 3-6 peppers of any color
  • 2 large onions

Instructions:

  1. First, slice up all the peppers and onions into strips.
  2. Cut the sausages into bite-sized pieces.
  3. Saute the onions for a minute, then add the peppers till soft.
  4. Throw your sausages in and cook until the sausage pieces are cooked all the way through.
